Okeaetos

Devious and deadly, the enigmatic Arakkighast killer known as Okeaetos is a frightful tangle of bony limbs and shadowy menace.

A terrifying mixture of flesh and bone, the Arakkighast are a race not often encountered in the Realm of Mythoss. Keeping mostly to unlit corners and hidden recesses, the assassin Okeaetos takes great joy in hunting down and eliminating his prey. A devout worshipper of his faction’s dark god, Okeaetos hopes that his dark deeds will be rewarded upon Necronominus‘ return.
